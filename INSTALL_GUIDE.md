# How to Install Your AutoMateSystem POS

This application is a Progressive Web App (PWA), which means you can install it on your phone, tablet, or laptop to use it like a native app. Follow the instructions below for your device.

## On a Laptop or Desktop (Chrome, Edge)

1.  **Open the App in Your Browser:** Navigate to the application's URL.
2.  **Look for the Install Icon:** In the address bar (usually on the right side), you will see an icon that looks like a computer screen with a down arrow.
3.  **Click and Confirm:** Click this icon, and a prompt will appear asking if you want to install the app. Click "Install."
4.  **Launch the App:** The application will now be in your computer's app list (e.g., in the Start Menu on Windows) and can be launched directly from your desktop.

## On Android (Phones and Tablets)

1.  **Open in Chrome:** Navigate to the application's URL in the Chrome browser.
2.  **Add to Home Screen:** Tap the three-dot menu icon in the top-right corner to open the Chrome menu.
3.  **Select "Install App" or "Add to Home screen":** Tap this option in the menu.
4.  **Confirm:** A confirmation pop-up will appear. Tap "Add" or "Install."
5.  **Find it on Your Home Screen:** The app will now be on your device's home screen, just like any other app.

## On iOS (iPhones and iPads)

1.  **Open in Safari:** Navigate to the application's URL in the Safari browser. **You must use Safari for this.**
2.  **Tap the Share Icon:** This is the icon at the bottom of the screen that looks like a square with an arrow pointing up.
3.  **Scroll and Find "Add to Home Screen":** In the share menu, scroll down and tap on the "Add to Home Screen" option.
4.  **Confirm the Name:** You can customize the name that will appear on your home screen. Tap "Add" in the top-right corner.
5.  **Launch from Your Home Screen:** The app is now installed on your device and can be launched like any other app.
